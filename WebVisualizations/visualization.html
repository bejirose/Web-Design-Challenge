Summary: Latitude vs X

This website visualizes the weather analysis of 500+ cities across the world of varying distance from the equator. This data analysis used data from the OpenWeatherMap API.


Used Matplotlib to plot the impact of weather compared to the factors such as Temperature, Cloudiness, Humidity, and Wind Speed. The plots and the source data that are used for this analysis are provided in this site along with the explanation of the plot and the analysis.
